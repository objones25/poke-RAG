Title: Pokémon Move Reference
Pound is a Pokémon move that deals standard damage. This physical attack involves striking the adversary using forelegs, a tail, or similar appendages. The target is physically assaulted with a long tail, a foreleg, or something comparable.

Karate Chop is a Pokémon move. It deals regular damage. The user's critical hit rate increases by one stage when employing this attack, making critical hits land more frequently. This sharp chopping strike has a high critical-hit ratio. This move is unusable and should be forgotten, as it cannot be relearned once dismissed.

Double Slap is a Pokémon move. It deals standard damage, striking the opponent between two and five times in a single turn. There is a 3/8 likelihood for this attack to connect twice or thrice, and a 1/8 likelihood for it to connect four or five times, resulting in an average of three hits per execution. The target is repeatedly struck with a back-and-forth motion. This move is currently unusable; it is advised to forget it, as it cannot be restored once dismissed.

Comet Punch is a Pokémon move that inflicts regular damage. This attack delivers a barrage of blows, hitting the target 2 to 5 times within one turn. The probability of landing 2 or 3 hits is 3/8 for each, while the chance for 4 or 5 hits is 1/8 for each, averaging 3 strikes per execution. The adversary is assailed by a rapid succession of punches. This move cannot be utilized and should be forgotten, as it is impossible to recall once dismissed.

Mega Punch is a Pokémon move that causes standard damage. This incredibly powerful strike unleashes a forceful blow. The target is heavily impacted by a punch delivered with immense, muscle-packed might.

Pay Day is a Pokémon move that deals regular damage. This move involves hurling numerous coins at the target to inflict harm. Following the conclusion of the battle, the victor obtains additional money, calculated as five times the user’s level for each successful use of this move.

Fire Punch is a Pokémon move that delivers standard damage. This attack features a fiery fist that strikes the target. It carries a $effect_chance% probability of inflicting a burn on the adversary.

Ice Punch is a Pokémon move, inflicting regular damage. This move strikes with an icy fist, and there is a $effect_chance% chance that the target will be left frozen as a result.

Thunder Punch is a Pokémon move that deals standard damage. The user attacks with an electrified fist, which carries a $effect_chance% chance to inflict paralysis upon the target.

Scratch is a Pokémon move that inflicts regular damage. The user employs its hard, pointed, and sharp claws to rake the target, causing harm.

Vice Grip is a Pokémon move, delivering regular damage. The user utilizes its huge, impressive, and powerful pincers to firmly grip and squeeze the target from both sides, inflicting damage.

Guillotine is a Pokémon move. This vicious, tearing pincer attack causes damage equivalent to the target’s maximum HP, leading to instant fainting upon impact. It disregards accuracy and evasion modifications. The move's inherent accuracy starts at 30%, increasing by 1% for every level the user surpasses the target; it will fail if the user's level is lower. While Endure can still prevent the target from fainting, effects like Lock On, Mind Reader, and No Guard apply if the user's level is equal to or greater than the target's, though they will not allow this move to bypass Detect or Protect.

Razor Wind is a Pokémon move that deals regular damage. This two-turn attack requires the user to prepare on the first turn before unleashing blades of wind on opposing Pokémon during the second turn. Its critical hit rate is elevated by one stage, making critical hits more frequent. This move cannot be chosen via Sleep Talk. Furthermore, this move is unusable and it is advised to forget it, as it cannot be relearned once abandoned.

Swords Dance is a Pokémon move. Through a frenetic and uplifting fighting dance, the user sharply boosts its offensive capabilities, raising its Attack stat by two stages.

Cut is a Pokémon move that inflicts regular damage. This basic attack involves the target being sliced with sharp claws, scythes, or similar implements. Beyond combat, it also possesses the utility to cut down thin trees and grass.
