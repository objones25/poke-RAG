Title: Pokémon Tier Formats Overview

**Ou format (BW):** Smogon's foundational usage-based classification is OU (OverUsed). All Pokémon are eligible for use within OU, excluding those banished to the Uber tier, and this includes species from UU, RU, and NU. However, while all non-Uber Pokémon may be utilized in OU, the 'OU' designation is specifically assigned only to those comprising at least ~3.41% of the total weighted usage within the tier. A Pokémon is truly 'OU' if a typical competitive player faces a greater than 50% likelihood of encountering it at least once over a standard day of 20 battles.

**Uu format (BW):** UU (UnderUsed) is Smogon's secondary usage-driven tier. Any Pokémon not classified as OU by usage and not banned to Ubers or UUBL may be deployed in UU, which encompasses Pokémon from RU and NU. Despite the eligibility of any aforementioned Pokémon, only those that achieve a minimum of ~3.41% of the tier's total weighted usage are formally designated UU. For a Pokémon to be genuinely UU, a typical competitive player must have a probability exceeding 50% of encountering it at least once across a full day of 20 matches.

**Nu format (BW):** Smogon's fourth tier, determined by usage, is NU (Neverused). This tier permits all Pokémon not ranked as OU, UU, or RU by usage, and not prohibited in Ubers, UUBL, RUBL, or NUBL; this includes Pokémon from PU. While all eligible Pokémon can be used in NU, the 'NU' designation applies solely to those constituting at least ~3.41% of the total weighted usage within the tier. A Pokémon is truly 'NU' if a typical competitive player has a better than 50% chance of encountering it at least once during a day's worth of 20 battles. Players are forbidden from using Pokémon from the Uber tier, OU, UUBL, UU, RUBL, RU, or NUBL.

**Uber format (BW):** Ubers stands as the most expansive of Smogon's tiers, facilitating the inclusion of any Pokémon species. Ubers remains unaffected by Pokémon usage in OU, UU, RU, or NU, and conversely, the usage of Pokémon within Ubers has no bearing on the compositions of those tiers.

**Pu format (BW):** Trainers are barred from deploying Pokémon from the Uber tier, OU, UUBL, UU, RUBL, RU, NUBL, or NU. The BW PU resources thread acts as the central hub on the forums for all BW PU-related discussions, offering materials such as sample teams, viability rankings, and speed tiers. Additionally, the Pokémon Showdown! PU room and the PU Discord server serve as excellent platforms for discussing and asking questions about the BW PU metagame.

**Ru format (BW):** RU (RarelyUsed) is Smogon's third usage-based tier. All Pokémon not already in UU or higher by usage, and not subject to bans in Ubers, UUBL, or RUBL, are eligible for use in RU, including Pokémon from NU. While any of these specified Pokémon can be utilized, only those that account for at least ~3.41% of the total weighted usage in the tier are officially designated RU. A Pokémon meets the true RU definition if a typical competitive player has a greater than 50% chance of encountering it at least once over 20 battles in a day. Players are not permitted to use Pokémon from the Uber tier, OU, UUBL, UU, or RUBL.

**Ou format (XY):** OU (OverUsed) functions as Smogon's primary usage-based tier. With the sole exception of Pokémon relegated to the Uber tier, every Pokémon is available for use in OU, encompassing those from UU, RU, NU, and PU. Notwithstanding the broad eligibility of all non-Uber Pokémon, the 'OU' classification is assigned only to species that achieve at least ~3.41% of the tier's total weighted usage. A Pokémon is genuinely OU if a typical competitor has a chance exceeding 50% of encountering it at least once during a day's worth of 20 battles.

**Uu format (XY):** UnderUsed (UU) represents Smogon's second usage-based tier. All Pokémon not categorized as OU by usage, and not explicitly banned to Ubers or UUBL, are permissible in UU, including contenders from RU, NU, and PU. Despite the aforementioned Pokémon's eligibility, only those accumulating at least ~3.41% of the total weighted usage within the tier are officially designated 'UU.' A Pokémon is truly 'UU' if a typical competitive player is more than 50% likely to encounter that Pokémon at least once in a given day of playing (20 battles). The UU Simple Questions, Simple Answers thread offers an excellent platform for posing any straightforward inquiries regarding the UU metagame; it is advised not to initiate new threads for questions answerable there. The UU Analyses forum is the appropriate venue for authoring analyses; one may reserve an analysis within the Analysis Reservation Thread or provide feedback on analyses that are a WIP. The Next Best Thing project focuses on a distinct Pokémon each week, endeavoring to uncover novel and effective sets for it. The UU Teambuilding Compendium project seeks to construct an efficient team for the UU metagame through community contributions and voting. Iron Chef: Smogon UU Edition presents a challenge to assemble a team centered around a specific concept and compete against other successful players.

**Nu format (XY):** The use of Pokémon from the Uber tier, OU, UUBL, UU, RUBL, RU, or NUBL is forbidden for players in this format. The NU Information thread provides data concerning the NU council along with the outcomes of Suspect tests and quickbans for the NU metagame. For simple questions pertaining to the NU metagame, the NU Simple Questions, Simple Answers thread serves as an ideal location; users should refrain from creating new threads for inquiries that can be addressed there. The NU Analyses forum is the designated space for writing analyses; reservations can be made in the Index Thread, or comments can be offered on analyses currently in progress. The NU Creative / Underrated Sets thread aims to identify less common but viable strategies within the NeverUsed metagame.

**Uber format (XY):** Smogon's most encompassing tier, Ubers, permits the deployment of any Pokémon species. Only abilities and tactics definitively proven to be uncompetitive face prohibition. Ubers remains unaffected by Pokémon usage in OU, UU, RU, NU, or PU, and conversely, Pokémon usage in Ubers exerts no influence on the formation of those tiers.

**Pu format (XY):** Players are barred from utilizing Pokémon from the Uber tier, OU, UUBL, UU, RUBL, RU, NUBL, NU, or PUBL. The ORAS PU resources thread acts as the central point on the forums for ORAS PU-related discussions, providing resources such as example teams, viability rankings, a role compendium, and speed tiers. Both the Pokémon Showdown! PU room and the PU Discord server are excellent venues for engaging in discussions and asking questions about the ORAS PU metagame.

**Ru format (XY):** RU (Rarely Used) constitutes Smogon's third usage-dependent tier. Any Pokémon not ranked as UU or higher by usage, and not forbidden in Ubers, UUBL, or RUBL, may be used in RU, including those originating from NU and PU. While such eligible Pokémon are permitted, only those representing at least ~3.41% of the total weighted usage in the tier earn the 'RU' designation. A Pokémon is authentically 'RU' if a typical competitive player has a greater than 50% likelihood of encountering it at least once during a 20-battle daily session. In addition to its dedicated subforum, RU also boasts a highly active and public room on Pokemon Showdown! Players are forbidden from selecting Pokémon from the Uber tier, OU, UUBL, UU, or RUBL.
